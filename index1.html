First post

Posted on 22nd of January

On the New Year Eve i decided to make gingerbread house. Well, i made a dough without any problems but the icing caused a lot of stress. Firstly, i spoiled the whole kitchen with sugar and tried to hold my mom away till i clean the kitchen because she does not like mess at all. Secondly, we had not any color, so it ended up being white. Very boring indeed. After pulling myself together, i started creating my masterpiece at least i thought i am creating a masterpiece which would be later demonstrated at the museum :)

Second post

Funny part of this story is that there was not any gingerbread house at the end. I just made gingerbread cookies with snow-white icing. I made a lot of figures: snowmen, gingerbread family, trees and mushrooms. Yes, mushrooms. it has no connection with New Year but i had the mushroom form.
